USAGE:

1- From your device get the folders RocketDatabases and RocketDownloads. It's locaion may vary with your iOS version and wether you're jailbroken or not. It will be inside Instgagram folder, you may use Filza or any file transfer on PC/Mac like iMazing, 3uTools etc.

2- Create a new folder and paste both folders you copied from your device. Also place RocketName.exe in this folder.

The structure must be:

-MyFolder
--RocketDatabases
--RocketDownloads
--RocketName.exe

3- Run RocketName.exe and it's done.

Warining: It will delete thumbnails by default.
